ABOUT US

_________

Everyone has problems, insecurities, unique experiences,

and difficulties in their lives that ultimately

shape who they are as a person. With this in mind,

we aim to change the way people think about their

lives in comparison to others and to bring

people closer together with all mediums of art.

We are going to spread a message that is universal:

that nobody is perfect and that’s perfectly fine.
